slick-spatial Demo

You can search for districts in Berlin/Germany. We have a database with spatial features enabled in the background. So either we can tell you to which district a coordiate belongs or we can tell you which districts are within a given rectangle.

find-by-coordinates

Insert your coordinates and we'll show you the district of Berlin this point is in.

Maybe you want to try out a predefined coordinate: Blücherstraße 22

find-by-bounding-box

Insert the boundaries of your rectangle and we'll show you all districts of Berlin contained in this rectangle.
